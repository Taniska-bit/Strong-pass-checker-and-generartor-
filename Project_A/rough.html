Password Strength Checker and Strong Password Suggester

A powerful desktop application designed to help you create and manage secure passwords with ease. With a simple and intuitive interface, users can quickly check the strength of their passwords and receive clear feedback on how to improve them. The app uses custom-built algorithms (no AI or online services) to analyze password complexity, ensuring your data stays private and secure. Additionally, the built-in strong password suggester generates robust, random passwords to help protect your accounts from unauthorized access. Take control of your online security with this fast, offline, and user-friendly solution for Windows, macOS, and Linux desktops.
